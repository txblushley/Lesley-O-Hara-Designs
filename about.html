About us


Based in Farnham, Surrey, and the surrounding area, we bring the joy of art directly to your child's school. Each action-packed, one-hour session is a whirlwind of artistic exploration, where we dive into a variety of techniques. From painting and drawing to the use of textiles, there's something for everyone!

For those budding designers and fabric fanatics, Lesley also offers fantastic Textile Clubs! These sessions introduce children to the wonderful world of fabrics. Get ready to create unique and colourful masterpieces!

Lesley O'Hara

Lesley O’Hara is a highly creative individual with a passion for art and design. Her journey began at the Kent Institute of Art and Design, where she completed a Foundation course, exploring various disciplines such as life drawing, painting, and sculpture. This foundation laid the groundwork for her subsequent studies at Surrey College of Art and Design, culminating in a BA Hons in Textiles.

Following her graduation, Lesley embarked on a successful career as a freelance textile designer for Rowena Bristow Studio. Her designs, characterized by their illustrative style, intricate geometries, and delicate florals, found global acclaim, gracing fabrics in Italy, France, Germany, Japan, Korea, UK and the United States. As Studio Manager, Lesley not only produced exceptional designs but also mentored and guided other aspiring designers.



Driven by a desire to share her passion for art with younger generations, Lesley established several engaging and inspiring Art Clubs and Textiles Clubs for primary school children. With a focus on hands-on learning and creative exploration, her clubs have become immensely popular, providing a nurturing environment for young artists to flourish.
