At Lesley O’Hara Designs, we believe in protecting the privacy of our young artists. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information on our website.

Information We Collect: We only collect the minimum amount of information necessary to provide a fun and safe experience. From Parents: We may collect a parent’s name, email address, and phone number to handle registrations or send newsletters. From Children: We do not require children to provide personal information to browse our site. If a child submits art for a gallery, we only display their first name and age/year (with parental consent).

How We Use Information: We use the information provided solely for: Processing club and event registrations. Sending updates about upcoming clubs Displaying kids artwork in our online gallery. Internal record-keeping for safety and emergency contacts

Parental Consent & Rights: We will never knowingly collect personal data from a child under 13 without verifiable parental consent. Parents have the right to: Review the information we have collected about their child. Request that we delete their child’s information at any time. Refuse further collection or use of their child's information.

Third-Party Sharing: We never sell, rent, or trade your child’s information to third parties or advertisers. We only share data with trusted service providers (like our email platform or registration form) strictly to run the club.

Security: We take security seriously and use industry-standard encryption to protect all data collected. However, please remember that no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Contact Us: If you have any questions about our privacy practices, please contact us at: Email: lesley.artclub@gmail.com
